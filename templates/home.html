OUR MOTTO

Our Product

Our solution measures shifts in sentiment from customer experience to win back consumers and activate fans. Using machine-powered learning and precise targeting, Apptentive helps enterprise brands capture actionable emotions and feedback from the 90 percent of consumer voices typically missed, and quickly turns that feedback into action.

Our Mission

We believe relationships matter. That’s why we’re on a mission to give a voice to every consumer and create better customer experiences for the world’s most popular brands.

How We Work

We hire people, not robots. That’s why we help you balance work with your family, friends, hobbies, and health. We pride ourselves on being adaptable because change is a constant. We make mistakes, learn, appreciate diverse thinking, and improve as we iterate.

A Voice for Everyone

We value diversity and strive to fill our office with people of different backgrounds and identities. Our vision is to give a voice to every customer as well as to every employee.
